About the Artist



I am currently a bachelor's student in Mathematics and Statistics at Concordia University in Montreal. Given my passion for coding, I like to take advantage of the university's computer science courses. Some of my favourites so far from Concordia and McGill have been
  • Concordia's web programming course (SOEN287),
  • McGill's functional programming course (COMP302),
  • McGill's COMP204 and COMP250 focused on Python and an overview of programming respectively,
  • Concordia's COMP248 and 249 which are sequential object-oriented programming courses in Java.


In addition to my student life, I like to make digital art, as seen on the main page of this concise portfolio. I am also an avid reader in my spare time.
